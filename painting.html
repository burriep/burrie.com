Painting Services

After blasting off old paint and rust, we can put on new durable coat that looks great. To make blasting most effective, it is essential to paint bare steel as soon as possible. Our paint bay is 25x60 ft. and is large enough to fit a semi-trailer inside, so chances are if you can get it here, we can paint it. The orange trailer in the picture was a project that we blasted and painted.

We use TNEMEC Phenolic Alkyd enamel and epoxy primers, the best in the industry, and finish the job with polyurethane enamel, industrial grade paint for unsurpassed durability and fade resistance. Our experienced painters are meticulous and will make sure that each job meets our high standards. We also provide restoration services on cars, trailers, trucks, and tractors.

Industrial Blasting and Painting

The clean professional appearance of your equipment can be restored through blasting and painting. At Burrie Enterprises, we have blasted and painted many industrial vehicles and equipment including dump trucks, semi-trailers, small trailers, loaders, and cranes. By keeping equipment looking new and well maintained, you can keep your professional appearance and show that you care for your equipment.

We offer many surface preparation services using a variety of media, including glass bead media, aluminum oxide, baking soda, and plastic media. We have a 40 ton crane on site for lifting.
